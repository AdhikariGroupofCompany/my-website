About Adhikari Group

Adhikari Group of Company is a diversified production-driven enterprise committed to quality, innovation, and sustainable growth. With a strong foundation in manufacturing high-quality plastic products, the group also operates across agriculture and other emerging business sectors.

Guided by modern technology, ethical business practices, and a customer-centric approach, we focus on delivering reliable products, supporting local communities, and expanding our multi-sector presence with long-term vision and responsibility.
